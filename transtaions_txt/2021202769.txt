Chapter II. - Supplement Temporary Unemployment Force majeure Coronfeldeling1. - amount of the supplement art. 2. The servants referred to in Article 1 who were temporarily unemployed in the Periodevan 1 July 2020 to 31 December 2020 due to force majeure Corona (code 77) are entitled to a supplement to the RVA benefit for temporary unemployment as a result of the Corona RVA benefit from the 8th day of 5 EUR gross per day, with a maximum of 52 (consecutive or not) days.
