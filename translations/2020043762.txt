This law regulates a matter as referred to in Article 74 of the Constitution. Art. 2. In Article 370 of the ordinary law of 16 July 1993 to complete the federal state structure, amended by the law of 7 March 1996, the provision under 8 ° is replaced: "8 ° ungempted fruit juices (including the grape) and ungusted vegetable juices , without added alcohol, with or without added sugar or other sweeteners of the GN code 2009, with the exception of freshly pressed fruit juices and vegetable juices that have not undergone any operation that are made in the retail trade on site and that are immediately offered for sale pre -consumption and which are therefore not intended for resale; ". Art. 3. In Article 7 of the Act of 21 December 2009 on the excise system of non -alcoholic drinks and coffee, amended on the Program Act of 25 December 2017, the provision under h) is replaced as follows: "h) Unguistic fruit juices and ungusted vegetable juices, without added alcohol, without added alcohol , with or without added sugar or other sweeteners of the GN code 2009, with the exception of freshly squeezed fruit juices and vegetables that have not undergone any operation, which are made in the retail trade on site and that are immediately offered for sale for consumption and therefore not for be resale; " Announce this law, order that it will be troubled by the Belgian Official Gazette with the country's seal.
