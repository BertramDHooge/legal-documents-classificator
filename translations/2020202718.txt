Publication: 2020-06-22Numac: 2020202718federal Public Service Employment, Labor and Social Consultation15 June 2020.-Act to suspend the notice period for dismissals given for or during the period of temporary suspension of the performance of the employment contract due to force majeure pursuant to the COVID- 19-Crisisfilip, King of the Belgians, to all who are now and will have accepted our greeting. The Chamber of Representatives and we follow it follows: Article 1. This law regulates a matter as referred to in Article 74 of the Constitution. Art. 2. Both the employee and the employer can cancel the employment contract during the suspension of the implementation of the agreement due to temporary force majeure that the consequential of the measures taken by the government to limit the distribution of the Coronavirus COVID-19 before or during the suspension referred to in the first paragraph, the termination period runs during that suspension. In the event of termination given by the employer before or during the suspension referred to in the first paragraph, the notice period will stop during the suspension. The preceding of the previous paragraph runs the cancellation period if it was already on foot for 1Maart 2020.Art. 3. This law comes into effect on the day on which it is announced in the Belgian Official Gazette. Best this law, orders that it will be trampled by the Belgian Official Gazette with the Lands Seal.
