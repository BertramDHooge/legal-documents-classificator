Form requirement (s) Developing formal requirements have been met:- The Finance Inspectorate has given advice on 9 June 2020; - The Flemish minister competent for the Finance and Budget has given his approval on 12 June 2020s of the urgency no advice is requested from the Council Vanstate. The urgent nature is motivated so that the resources could still be granted in the current school and academic year so that the most urgent needs can be yellowed. Motoring disposal decision is based on the following motive: the Coronacrisis and the associated federal measures a major impact on Flemish Education, also on a budgetary level. Primary and secondary schools are confronted with the fact that parents ask for financial compensation for canceled school trips - travel and with the fact that they have lost income that is a supplement to the regular operating resources. The student facilities within higher education institutions are confronted with student with serious financial issues. The present decision wants educational institutions financial supports for this financial compensation to parents and/or the lost income and/or the support that they offer students with financial problems. School boards of the ordinary and extraordinary primary and secondary education are additional operating budget for the 2019-2020 school year as compensation on the one hand for parents or persons involved for canceled school trips-travel, on the other for schools for lost income that have not been able to take place or for reduced income . Schools must have worked out a reimbursement system for Costendie parents for these extramuros activities. Art. 2. The amount per school is calculated as follows:- a fixed amount per student in ordinary and special-bearing education of 5.16 euros .- A fixed amount per student in ordinary and extraordinary secondary education and part-time vocational secondary education of 12.88 euros . For the calculation the following counting date applicable: 1 February 2020.Art. 3. For the Higher Education Institutions, an additional social allowance of 684,000 euros for universities and 816,000 euros for the colleges is awarded for the 2019-2020 academic year. This amount is included on the institutions in accordance with the distribution mechanism in the articles III.68 and III.69 of the Codex higher education. The institutions support students who have financial problems as a result of the COVID-19 crisis. Art. 4. This decision takes effect on the date of the approval.art.5.
