Publication: 2020-06-24Numac: 2020041861 Ministerie of the French Community 13 June 2020.-Decree of the French Community on the meetings organized by the public administration school for public administration in the context of the Health Crisis COVID-19TE Government of the French Community, in view of the decree of 17 March2020 granting special powers to the government in the context of the health crisis COVID-19, Article 1, § 1, g); Having regard to the decree of 20 December 2011 of the French community approval of the cooperation agreement of 10 November 2011 between the French Community and Hetwaal's region to set up a joint school for public administration for the French Community the Walloon Region; having regard to the decision of the government of the French Community of 19 April2012 on the travel allowances and the attendance fees For the presidents and members of the board of directors and the scientific Municipal Executive of Public Administration that the French Community has in common; that as a result of the restriction measures taken in the context of the fight against COVID-19, the bodies of the public administration school are brought together and make decisions according to methods that make it possible to avoid physical contact between persons; Such methods do not explicitly provide for the rules established in the aforementioned cooperation agreement of 10 November 2011 and in the internal regulations; Meeting is attended by several people in remote locations, connected to each other by one or more telecommunications, such as a telephone conference, video conference and online conference; whereas the need to determine a decision that makes validation possible per teleconference are kept and made of decisions there since March 18, 2020; whereas the Walloon government by decision no. 25 of 27 April 2020 of special powers concerning the meetings of the Walloon government bodies, the allocation of seat fees for the meetings of the organs of these bodies has maintained a case of participation in meetings via teleconference; that this decision applies to the school for public administration; on the nomination of the Minister of Civil Service, after-date, decision: Article 1. Electronic consultation of documents is permitted the meetings and vote and all decision-making within the decision-making and advisory bodies of the school For public administration, it can take place per teleconference.Art. 2. in the event of defeat provisions in a decree, a decision, a house rules or in the public administration school: 1 ° the person responsible for the convened decision to organize a meeting per teleconference; 2 ° ensures the Person Diede Meetings, for the most suitable moment that a quorum is present to make a valid decision; 3 ° the grant of attendance fees as provided for meetings of the organs of the public administration school is maintained in the event of participation in meetings that per teleconference are held.art. 3. This decree decision has elaboration with effect from 18 March 2020 and stops being in force on 31 December 2020.brussel, 18 June 2020.voorde Government: the Prime Minister, P.-Y.
