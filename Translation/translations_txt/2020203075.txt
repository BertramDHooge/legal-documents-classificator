Publication: 2020-07-16NUMAC: 2020203075WAAL GOVERNAL SERVICE 30 June 2020.-Ministerial Decree in which the measures bound by the COVID-19 crisis and the temporary prohibition on imposing restrictions in access to the public water distribution are extended by one month Environment, Nature, Forests, National Affairs and Animal Welfare, In view of the Decreeing Part of Book II of the Environmental Book, which entails the Water Code, Articles D.202 and D.232; Having been used to have the Water Code , Article R.270BIS-13; In view of the decision of the Walloon Government of 22 April 2020 on the temporary prohibition on imposing restrictions in access to public water distribution due to the urgent measures taken to limit the distribution of the Coronavirus COVID-13 ; In view of the duration of the consequences of the health crisis related to COVID-19 and the enforcement of various measures to limit the spread of the virus under the population, which require permanent and sufficient access to water; in view of the enforcement of certain restrictions on activities and travel within the framework of the Federal Afbouwplancovid-19, Decree: Article 1. The consequences of the measures taken in the decision of the Walloon Government of 22 April 2020 on the temporary prohibition on imposing restrictions in access to public water distribution for the Emergency measures taken to limit the distribution of the Coronavirus COVID-19, will be extended until July 31, 2020. Art. 2. Every water distributor of Wallonia is notified of this decision. 3. This Decree works the day on which it is signed. Names, 30 June 2020. C. Tellier
